ABOUT US

FACTOR-EMNAS ENTERPRISES CORP. is a full-service engineering firm specializing in the supply, installation, repair and maintenance of fire detection and alarm systems, fire protection solutions, HVAC and air-conditioning systems, electrical works, roofing, solar-powered installations, and reinstatement services.

With a commitment to safety, reliability, and technical excellence, we partner with commercial, industrial and institutional clients to protect assets, restore and upgrade building systems, optimize performance, and support sustainable energy initiatives.

  • Quality & Reliability
  • Technical Excellence
  • Customer-Centric Service
Our Services

Our Mission

  • Provide end-to-end engineering and reinstatement services backed by experienced professionals, best-in-class equipment and rigorous quality controls.
  • Enhance occupant safety through cutting-edge fire detection, alarm and suppression systems.
  • Maximize energy efficiency and indoor comfort with customized HVAC, air-conditioning and solar power solutions.
  • Deliver prompt, cost-effective maintenance and reinstatement works to ensure uninterrupted operations and safe, functional spaces.
  • Uphold the highest standards of integrity, compliance and customer care in every project.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted engineering partner—delivering integrated life-safety, comfort and energy solutions that set the standard for innovation, quality and environmental stewardship.

Core Values

  • Safety First: We design, restore and maintain systems that protect lives and property.
  • Quality & Reliability: We adhere to industry best practices and manufacturer guidelines.
  • Technical Excellence: Our engineers and technicians pursue continuous training and certification.
  • Customer-Centricity: We listen, collaborate and tailor solutions to each client's needs.
  • Sustainability: We promote energy-efficient, eco-friendly and cost-effective building restoration solutions.

Our Approach

  • Collaborative Planning: We engage stakeholders early to align on objectives, budgets and schedules.
  • Detailed Engineering: Our CAD-driven designs and simulations minimize risk and change orders.
  • Stringent Quality Control: On-site supervision and third-party inspections ensure compliance.
  • Transparent Communication: Clear progress reporting and flexible change-management processes.
  • After-Sales Support: Dedicated service team available for troubleshooting, repairs and preventive care.

Our Services

Comprehensive engineering solutions tailored to your needs

Reinstatement Services

  • Dismantling & Removal, Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing Works
  • Fire Protection Works, Final Cleaning & Turnover
  • Reinstatement of walls, ceilings, floors, finishes
  • Structural & architectural restoration
  • Painting, plastering, tiling, re-cabling
  • Compliance to building codes & safety standards

Fire Detection & Alarm Systems

  • Design of addressable & conventional systems
  • Supply of detectors, control panels, notification devices
  • Integration with building management systems (BMS)
  • Testing, commissioning and code-compliance documentation

Fire Protection

  • Automatic sprinkler and water-mist systems
  • Fire extinguishers, standpipes and hose reel installations
  • Special hazard suppression (FM-200, CO₂, clean agents)
  • Inspection, testing and 24/7 emergency service

HVAC & Air Conditioning

  • Central and split-system design, installation and balancing
  • Chillers, cooling towers and air-handling units
  • Preventive maintenance and seasonal tune-ups
  • Indoor air quality assessment and solutions

Electrical Works

  • Low- and medium-voltage distribution, lighting and power systems
  • Switchgear, panelboards and backup generators
  • Structured cabling and data/telecom infrastructure
  • Energy audits and power factor correction

Solar Panel Systems

  • Site assessment, system design and feasibility studies
  • Grid-tie and off-grid PV installations
  • Monitoring, maintenance and performance optimization
